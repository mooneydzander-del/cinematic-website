The Build Process

How Every Cinema Project Gets Built

Six deliberate phases — each one designed to keep you informed, in control, and confident in the outcome.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a deep-dive conversation about your business, your goals, your competitors, and your target customer. This isn't a sales call — it's intelligence gathering that shapes everything that follows.

02

Scope & Strategy

Based on discovery, we map out the exact scope, page structure, conversion strategy, and technical requirements. You get a clear proposal with a fixed price, fixed timeline, and zero guesswork.

03

Design

We design every page from scratch — custom layouts, typography hierarchy, motion concepts, and full visual direction. You review and approve before a single line of code is written. No surprises at launch.

04

Development

Clean, performant code — no bloated builders, no unnecessary dependencies. GSAP animations, smooth scroll, lead capture systems, SEO structure, and full mobile optimization baked in from the start.

05

Review & Refinement

You review the live staging environment and submit all feedback in one consolidated round. We refine until it's exactly right — then lock it for launch. Scope is honored. Quality is guaranteed.

06

Launch & Handoff

We deploy, configure your domain, run final performance checks, and hand over everything you own — code, credentials, documentation. You're equipped to run it, and we're available if you need more.

Expectations

What You Can Count On

Every Cinema project runs the same way — because predictability is part of the premium product.

↗

Fixed Prices

What we quote is what you pay. No scope creep billing, no change-order surprises mid-project. The price is locked when you sign — and that's the number on the invoice.

↗

Clear Timelines

Every project comes with a milestone schedule. You'll know the design deadline, the dev deadline, the review window, and the launch date — all before we start.

↗

Direct Communication

You talk to the people building your site. No account managers buffering your feedback. Fast responses, honest answers, and no bureaucracy between you and your deliverables.

↗

Full Ownership at Launch

You leave with everything: source files, domain control, hosting credentials, and the ability to hand it to any developer going forward. It's your asset — completely.
