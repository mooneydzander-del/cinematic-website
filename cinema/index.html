Common Questions

Questions Business Owners Usually Ask

Why not just send ads to my regular website?

You can, but regular websites are built for general information. A landing page is built around one offer and one action, which makes it significantly better for ad campaigns where you want visitors to do something specific.

Do I need a landing page if I already have a website?

Yes, especially if you are running ads. Your website can still exist for everything else, but your ads should send people to a focused page built specifically for that campaign and offer.

Can the page collect quote requests or bookings?

Yes. The page can include a contact form, quote request form, click-to-call button, booking link, email notification, or other lead capture method based on what your business needs.

How fast can a landing page be built?

Simple landing pages can usually be built quickly once the offer, business details, and needed content are ready. More advanced pages with funnels, integrations, or cinematic motion effects take longer depending on complexity.

Will the cinematic effects slow the page down?

The goal is to keep every page smooth, fast, and conversion-focused. Motion is used to guide attention toward the offer and CTA — not to make the page harder to use or load.

Can this work for local businesses?

Yes. Landing pages work especially well for local service businesses because they help people quickly understand the offer and take action — whether that is a call, booking, or quote request.

Do you write the words for the page?

Yes. Cinema helps create persuasive landing page copy including the headline, benefits, trust sections, FAQs, and calls to action — written to move visitors toward becoming a lead.
